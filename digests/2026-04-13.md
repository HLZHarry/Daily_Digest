## 💡 Worth Your Time / 今日精读

**[Mythos, Muse, and the Opportunity Cost of Compute](https://stratechery.com/2026/mythos-muse-and-the-opportunity-cost-of-compute/)** — Stratechery

Ben Thompson's framing here is genuinely useful for anyone thinking about AI platform dynamics: as GPU capacity becomes the scarce input, the question of *who directs demand* becomes strategically more important than who owns the infrastructure. For a pension fund allocator evaluating AI infrastructure investments versus AI application-layer bets, this distinction between supply-side and demand-side moats is worth sitting with carefully.

Ben Thompson的这篇分析对思考AI平台竞争格局很有价值：当GPU算力成为稀缺要素，"谁引导需求"在战略上比"谁拥有基础设施"更重要。对于在AI基础设施投资与应用层投注之间做权衡的养老金配置者而言，供给侧护城河与需求侧护城河的区分值得深入思考。
